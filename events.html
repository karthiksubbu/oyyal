    OYYAL FIELD VISIT TO 2 NEW TRIBAL VILLAGES - RAMANDODDI & CHINNA GUTHTHI : KRISHNAGIRI 30 September 2019 In the pursuit of its mission of socio economic development in Rural areas , OYYAL has always been enthusiastic to explore new horizons.
