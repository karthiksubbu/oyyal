about us
  • OYYAL, in Classical Tamil,means “TO ELEVATE”.
  • OYYAL, as the name suggests, strives for the elevation of the society and people, choosing education in rural areas as a means.
  • OYYAL works for the empowerment of rural people and students by facilitating the education and development by various means.
  • OYYAL firmly believes that, educating a person per family can seed a revolution in the whole family, for generations to come and thus changing into a socio development factor.

ORIGIN

OYYAL was seeded in the year 2012, by a group of likeminded young professionals from various parts and various backgrounds currently living in Bangalore. The Oyyalites, who were associated with various social organizations in prior, were chorded by the quest of striving for social well-being and with a motive to impact the environs they live.

Oyyal is a registered Public Charitable trust (Reg no: 57/14) by the Government of Tamil Nadu.

Place and means of work:

  • OYYAL focuses on the ‘Rural education’ as one of the key areas of work for the under-privileged students
  • OYYAL also works for the socio-economic development of the rural people by working with the people and the Government to avail the welfare schemes
  • At present, the land chosen for our work are the remote villages in Krishnagiri District, Tamil Nadu, which includes a few tribal villages as well

Mission

Our objective is to empower the society by insisting on the importance of education to rural people and students.

  • Nurture the knowledge and skill base of the students by forming a learning center in each village
  • Eliminating drop outs at High school
  • Arrange for guest lectures, focusing on career guidance, exam preparation, and nurturing extracurricular skills
  • Enabling students to go for Higher education after High School
  • Support for maintaining infrastructure at government schools
  • Arrange for temporary teachers, for key subjects at government schools
  • Prevention of Child labour and Child Marriages
  • Ensuring Child Protection
  • Guiding and helping the rural people to avail the government welfare schemes and thereby enabling them to progress economically

VISION

  • To Elevate the Society through Education
  • Attaining Good Quality Education for all, including the unprivileged sections in the society
  • To Create Socially and economically healthy and self-sustained villages
